Fresh Brew Plus Thermal Carafe Coffee Maker EC-YTC100
Brewing flavorful coffee is easy with our Fresh Brew Plus Thermal Carafe Coffee Maker, which features a vacuum insulated stainless steel thermal carafe to keep coffee hot for hours without the 'burnt' flavor caused by heating plates.
  • 200°F high brewing temperature heats water to the right temperature to brew flavorful coffee (when brewing using Time Saver mode)
  • Pre-infusion cycle evenly wets coffee grounds before brewing, allowing them to bloom for perfect extraction of flavor and aroma
  • Special design feature for brewing iced coffee—iced coffee water measure lines adjust the water-to-coffee ratio for a stronger coffee that doesn't taste watered down when diluted with ice
  • 24-hour programmable timer with digital clock delays brewing for fresh coffee in the morning
  • Sound Signal can be switched to Mute for quieter operation
  • BPA-free (all surfaces that come into contact with food or beverage)
  • cULus listed
  • Instruction Manual in English and French
