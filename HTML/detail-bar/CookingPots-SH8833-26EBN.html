Product detail

Anodizing is an electrochemical process that forms a protective coating of aluminum oxide on the aluminum surface and results a strong aluminum connection called Anodized aluminum. The Anodized aluminum layer is as hard as diamond and widely used in the production of automotive, aircraft, computer industries…
Applying this technology - SUNHOUSE offers the Anodized cookware set with long lasting durability and delicate, elegant design that is the perfect choice for every home kitchen.
Elegant & luxury design suitable for modern kitchen
  • Shiny and stainless surface: Anodized pot is produced by modern coated aluminum technology, qualified machining which make products have smooth, shiny surface and easy to clean. Anodized technology helps to avoid pitted surface in case of containing hot or sour food in comparison with common aluminum pot.
  • Lid: Made from two layer 5mm thick heat-resistant and transparent glass which is convenient for cooking as well as observing food without opening the lid many times. The lid rim made of stainless steel helps to tighten the lid, limit cracking if impacted by force.
  • Handle/Knob: Made from high grade EB plastic with good heat resistant, heat insulation, anti-burning during cooking and moving. Handle riveted firmly to the pot body for easy lifting.
Excellent features make the cooking easier, simpler and quicker
  • Ultra-durable, ultra-light: The Anodized cookware has extremely high hardness and excellent abrasion resistance, eliminating scratches. The product can be used for a variety of cooking purposes and will not be deformed if impacted by force.
  • Fast cooking: Hard, thick and heat resistant anodized aluminum layer helps to ensure fast heat transfer and save cooking time.
  • Safe for health: Anodized pots are treated by oxidizing method so absolutely no exposure to food, safe for health. It also helps to keep the fresh taste of food in relative time.
  • Compatible with heat sources: Pot is compatible with gas stove, infrared cooker except induction cooker.
  • Suitable for small family of 3-5 members: 26 cm diameter and high wall are ideally for cooking soup, egg, meat, hot pot, especially boiling 2.0 kg chicken and suitable for 3-5 people.
