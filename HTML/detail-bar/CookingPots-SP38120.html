Anodizing is an electrochemical process that forms a protective coating of aluminum oxide on the aluminum surface and results a strong aluminum connection called Anodized aluminum. Applying this technology - SUNHOUSE offers the Anodized cookware set with long lasting durability and delicate, elegant design that is the perfect choice for every home kitchen.
Elegant & luxury design suitable for modern kitchen
  • Shiny and stainless surface: Product is made from hard anodized aluminum alloy with advanced technology which gives product hardness and durability, high corrosion resistance, high temperature resistance as well as good non-stick ability while retaining the excellent of aluminum as lightweight, well heat absorbability, fast heat transfer and even heat distribution.
