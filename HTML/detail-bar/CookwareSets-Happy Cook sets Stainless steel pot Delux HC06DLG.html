Product detail

Stainless steel pot set Happy Cook Delux HC06DLG made of high quality stainless steel material safe for health:
Stainless Cook pot set Happy Cook Delux HC06DLG made from stainless steel 304 (18/10) imported from South Korea, stainless, looks luxurious, keeps the gloss and does not stain after a long time of use.
Happy Cook pot set HC06DLG includes 3 different pots with a diameter of 16cm, 20cm, 24cm so depending on the purpose of use, you can choose the right pot to cook stir-fry, stock, or cook soup.
Happy Cook stainless steel pot HC06DLG has 3 layers of bottom structure including: the first stainless steel layer in contact with the kitchen surface to help heat well, the second core layer of aluminum helps to radiate heat around, the inner layer is stainless steel to help keep Good heat. So the 3-bottom pot has a fire protection function during cooking, saving fuel and keeping food hot longer.
Showmore
The lid is designed with heat-resistant glass, sealed to help keep the heat well and protect the taste of food long term. The air vents on the lid also prevent the food movement from cooking and the transparent color of the glass makes it easy to observe the food inside during the cooking process.
How to use and preserve stainless steel pots:
  • After use, you should clean and dry the pot to avoid water stains.
  • To prevent food from spilling out, you should cook it in accordance with the pot capacity.
  • Keep the lid closed during cooking (depending on the dish) to save gas and keep nutrients in food.
  • Do not pour cold water when the pot is hot to avoid sudden changes in the environment, reducing the pot life.
  • To clean the food that sticks to the bottom of the pot, add some water to boil and wait for it to cool and then scrub.
  • For stains or burns, you should soak the pot in water and then use a cleaning solution to clean it.
  • Do not leave the stainless steel pot with plastic straps in the microwave.
