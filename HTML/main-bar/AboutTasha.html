About us

Tasha Corporation

MISSION

Convenience to your kitchen

Our mission is to enhance lives by creating tools for a quiet, healthy and stylish home: appliances that are reliable, efficient and a joy to use – for more peace of mind in everyday life.

‘The new kitchen lifestyle’

We source innovation from most of our suppliers to ensure the products are innovative.The latest Intelligence of Things (IoT) Tracker digs into AI-infused appliances is a thing and smart appliances become the backbone for a ‘new kitchen lifestyle’

Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate social responsibility is core to our business. We always take care of domestic job growth, human rights, climate change and invest on educating our employees to be aware of their contribution on the community
