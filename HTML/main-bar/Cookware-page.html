Product Story

Cookware mostly uses aluminum. Aluminum is the most common silver and white soft metal and “light metal” that is safe and light, not the heavy metal that is harmful and accumulated in the body. Aluminum is easy to process and has a great heat conduction and corrosion resistance.
The product of household pot, aluminum pans adopting cold anodized technology is aluminum which is embedded in anodized tank. When the current passes through the anodized tank, the aluminum surface is oxidized. That oxidation surface will form an outer hard shell for aluminum, and then a powerful aluminum bonding called anodized aluminum will be born at the end of this process.
  • Aluminum anod has superior advantages over conventional aluminum cookware. Anodized hard anodized aluminum, high durability, good scratch resistance, shiny colors over time. The pot is not susceptible to distortion, heat resistance and good impact, no chemical reaction with food.
  • Anodizing technology can be used for dyeing of green, yellow, bronze ... (instead of pure silver white aluminum) but absolutely does not affect the food ...
  • Heat-fast cookers shorten cooking time.
  • Anod aluminum cookware is a high-end product line with elegant design, variety of products to choose from.
