Product Story

To choose a safe food container, there are 2 most important factors: material and temperature. Good material will give wide adaptability in many different temperature environments, even adapt well to acidic, acidic environment ...
  • The safest material is glass. Glass does not work with acidic substances, so acid does not produce toxic substances.
At the same time, the glass can withstand very high temperatures so it will be more versatile, can be used in refrigerators, microwaves, ovens ...
  • Absolutely do not choose floating plastic, do not specify the number of plastic, no clear origin.
  • Our company specializes in providing safe plastic food containers such as PP plastic (plastic No. 5), HDPE plastic (No. 2), LDPE plastic (No. 1) for food storage.
  • Covered food containers should be used when storing fresh items.
  • Absolutely do not use foam box, plastic number 7.
