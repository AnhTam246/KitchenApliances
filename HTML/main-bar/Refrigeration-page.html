Product Story

Are you looking for a practical, elegant food storage solution? Working with the best Freezer and Refrigerator brands in the industry like LG, Bosch,.. Tasha offers you with a range of various types of refrigerators and freezers that play as the most innovative place to keep your food in its fresh quality and provide spacious space to organize your groceries efficiently such as:
  • French door: a side by side top with a bottom freezer. Features that are designed for the items you use most and the room you need with a shelf that slides back to fit tall items. Purposeful spaces make organizing food easy
  • Side-by-side: offer easy access for the fridge and freezer section with proportionally more freezer space and much easier access to freezer than other types
  • Bottom-Freezer: Open your refrigerator to the left or the right for added convenience with the freezer under the fridge.
  • Top-Freezer: Frozen food is at eye level to find your favorites fast in this refrigerator option.
The products are designed to be consumer friendly that allow easy access when using. With our diverse offer, whether you’re looking for the latest refrigeration technology or need additional fresh or frozen food storage, we’re here to help you find the best appliances for your family.
